About

I have a thirst for knowledge and am learning more about how to code daily. I am excited to learn more languages and everything I can about web development. I truly have a passion for code and it's just something that I love doing as my career. I have built several websites and it gives me a feeling of great accomplishment when I see how happy the customers are with their finished product. I want to take my experience and knowledge to a team to create and maintain some truly dynamic websites and web applications. Although I may be a front-end Web Developer at the moment, I strive to be a full-stack developer eventually.


Last year I attended the LambdaConf 2015 conference in Boulder, Colorado which allowed me to be introduced to several other languages and frameworks, such as: Elm, PureScript, Clojure/ClojureScript, Haskell, Rust and many other concepts of functional programming. I also attended Develop Denver which is another conference on various programming languages along with classes on design and data.


I have also started working on some non-profit projects through Code For Denver where we work as a team and collaborate on how to get the project done. With each team member having experience in various languages and frameworks, we can all give our input and expertise to create something truly awesome. The main project I have been working on with Code for Denver is for the City of Denver. It's an energy sustainability project showing the buildings in Denver and whether they have an Energy Star Score rating if they are over 50,000 square feet. Here is the beta version of that project here: Denver Sustainability
